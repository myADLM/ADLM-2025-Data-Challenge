# I. Device Description:

The Clinitek Advantus Urinalysis analyzer is a semi-automated, bench top analyzer. It is designed to read Bayer Reagent Strips, such as Multistix 10 SG or Multistix PRO Reagent Strips.

The analyzer is a reflectance spectrophotometer that analyzes the color and intensity of the light reflected from the reagent area and reports the results. The analyzer can determine and report the color of the urine. The user can enter the clarity for each specimen. Calibration is performed automatically each time a reagent strip is run.

The analyzer has a touch screen display, printer, push bar and fixed platform which consist of strip loading station, incubation/read station and waste bin.

During testing a reagent strip is manually dipped into a urine sample then placed on the strip loading station. The push bar moves the strip to the incubation/read station, where it is tested. When testing is complete, the strip is dropped into the waste bin and the results are printed.
