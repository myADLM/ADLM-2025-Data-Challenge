# Device description:

The MSC Lipid Control is a human serum based liquid quality control containing stabilized HDL-C, LDL-C, CHOL, TRIG, Apo AI, and Apo B of human origin.

Material of human origin used in the manufacture of this MSC Lipid Control kit was tested at the donor level using FDA approved methods.

# Intended use:

The MSC Lipid Control is intended for use as an assayed quality control material to monitor the ongoing precision of clinical laboratory systems for the following analytes: High-density Lipoprotein Cholesterol (HDL-C), Low-density Lipoprotein Cholesterol (LDL-C), Total Cholesterol (CHOL), Triglycerides (TRIG), Apolipoprotein AI (Apo AI), and Apolipoprotein B (Apo B), on the instruments listed in the value sheet.
