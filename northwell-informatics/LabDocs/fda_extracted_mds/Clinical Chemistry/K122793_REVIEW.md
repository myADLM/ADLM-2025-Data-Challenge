# H. Intended Use:

1. Intended use(s): The DiaSorin LIAISON $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Testosterone is a direct, competitive, chemiluminescence immunoassay (CLIA) intended for the quantitative determination of testosterone in human serum and EDTA plasma on the LIAISON $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Analyzer. # L. Test Principle:

The method for quantitative determination of the LIAISON $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Testosterone assay is a direct, competitive, chemiluminescence immunoassay (CLIA). Specific antibody to testosterone is bound to magnetic particles (solid phase) and testosterone is linked to an isoluminol derivative. During the incubation, testosterone is dissociated from its binding protein and competes with labeled testosterone for binding sites on the antibody. After the incubation, the unbound material is removed with a wash cycle. Subsequently, the starter reagents are added and a flash chemiluminescent reaction is initiated. The light signal is measured by a photomultiplier as relative light units (RLU) and is inversely proportional to the concentration of testosterone present in calibrators, controls, or samples.

# d. Detection limit:

The Limit of Blank (LoB), Limit of Detection (LoD), and Limit of Quantitation (LoQ) were determined in accordance with CLSI document EP17-A. LoB was calculated using 5 blank samples tested on 2 LIAISON $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ analyzers over three days using 6 runs with two reagent lots. LoD was calculated using 4 low concentration samples, at and above the mean LoB, tested on 2 LIAISON $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ analyzers over 3 days using 6 runs and 2 reagent lots. LoQ was calculated using 8 samples tested on 2 LIAISON $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ analyzers over 3 days using 6 runs and 2 reagent lots.

The LoB was determined to be $3 . 1 \ \mathrm { n g / d L }$ and the LoD was determined to be $9 . 8 ~ \mathrm { n g / d L }$ . The LoQ, which was defined as the lowest concentration for which the CV is less than $20 \%$ ., was calculated to be $1 6 \mathrm { n g / d L }$ .

The claimed measuring range of the device is 16 to $1 5 0 0 \mathrm { n g / d L }$ .
