# Device Description:

The PTS Professional Chemistry Kit combines devices that are already cleared and packages them together for the convenience of the user.

There are two CardioChek¬Æ Analyzers: the CardioChek¬Æ PA Analyzer and the CardioChek¬Æ Plus Analyzer. These analyzers are part of portable test systems. The CardioChek¬Æ PA test system and the CardioChek¬Æ Plus test system consist of their respective analyzer and PTS Panels¬Æ Test Strips with a lotspecific MEMo¬Æ Chip.

The CardioChek¬Æ PA and Plus test systems are for the quantitative determination of glucose, total cholesterol, HDL (high density lipoprotein) cholesterol and triglycerides in venous whole blood and capillary whole blood from the fingertip and are intended for multiple patient uses in professional healthcare settings. These systems are for in vitro diagnostic use only.

The PTS Panels¬Æ Test Strips consist of varying amounts of test strips packed into a vial and a lot-specific MEMo¬Æ Chip. PTS Panels¬Æ Test Strips include reflectance and electrochemical test strips. The reflectance assays include quantitative determination of glucose, total cholesterol, HDL (high density lipoprotein) cholesterol and triglycerides. The electrochemical assay is for glucose measurement. The reflectance test strips are available with one, two, and three assays and come in various combinations of assays. The included PTS Panels¬Æ Test Strips include: PTS Panels¬Æ Lipid Panel Test Strips, PTS Panels¬Æ Chol+HDL $^ +$ Glu Test Strips, PTS Panels¬Æ Chol+HDL Test Strips, PTS Panels¬Æ Chol+Glu Test Strips, PTS Panels¬Æ eGlu Test Strips, PTS Panel¬Æ Glucose Test Strips, and CardioChek Plus Smart Bundle‚Ñ¢ Pack. The PTS Panels¬Æ Test Strips are single-use.

PTS Panels¬Æ Controls contain chemicals that react with test strips to produce color or an electrical current depending on the test strips used. Controls should be run to verify the test system performance, when results are questionable, to comply with a facility‚Äôs quality control requirements, or as required by local accrediting and regulatory bodies. They contain two vials of control solution that have two levels of each analyte. Controls included in this kit are the PTS Panels¬Æ HDL Cholesterol Controls and PTS Panels¬Æ Multi-Chemistry Controls.

The PTS Collect‚Ñ¢ Capillary Tubes are a glass and plastic tube capable of dispensing an exact volume of blood from the capillary tube to a test strip. The tubes are available with volumes of 15, 20, 30, and 40 $\mu \mathrm { L }$ .

The A1CNow $^ { \mathbb { B } } +$ test provides quantitative measurement of the percent of glycated hemoglobin (%A1C) levels in capillary (fingerstick) or venous whole blood samples. A1CNow $^ { \mathbb { B } } +$ consists of three components: a semi-disposable plastic-encased device (the analyzer), a plastic cartridge enclosing dry reagent strips, and a shaker kit. The units of the analyzer are pre-programmed on the analyzer and are available in either percentage of glycated hemoglobin $\%$ A1C) or millimoles per mole (mmol/mol). The analyzers are lot specific and intended to be discarded once the provided cartridges are consumed.

The Unistik¬Æ 3 Pre-set Single Use Safety Lancets are sterile, single-use, auto-disabling lancets to perform finger sticks to obtain capillary blood.
