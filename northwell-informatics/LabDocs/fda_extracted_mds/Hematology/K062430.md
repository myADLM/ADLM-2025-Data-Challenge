# Device Description:

Hemos Protein C is an in vitro diagnostic test for the quantitative determination of Protein Cin human citrated plasma based on a synthetic chromogenic substrate. Protein C deficiency is associated with recurrent venous thrombosis, especially in young adults. Acquired deficiencies of Protein C are associated with hepatic disorders, oral anticoagulant therapy and disseminated intravascular coagulation.
