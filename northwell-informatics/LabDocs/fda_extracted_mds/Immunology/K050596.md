The Quantia A1-AT is intended for the in vitro quantitative determination of alpha-1-antitrypsin concentration in human serum or plasma (heparin with or without gel separator, EDTA) on the AEROsETÂ® system as an aid in the diagnosis of juvenile and adult cirrhosis of the liver and pulmonary emphysema.

Quantia PROTEINS Control is intended for use in monitoring the quality control of results obtained with the Quantia A1-AT reagents by turbidimetry. (NOTE: This control has been also FDA 510(k) submitted for use with Quantia Beta-2 Microglobulin). For in vitro diagnostic use.

# Summary of Performance Data:

In a method comparison study evaluating 111 samples with alpha-1-antitrypsin levels ranging from 42.0 to 442.5 mg/dL on the Abbott AEROSET $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ instrument, the slope was 1.002 and the correlation coefficient (r) was 0.9890 for Quantia A1-AT versus the predicate device.
