# I. Device Description:

The N Antisera to Human Complement Factors (C3c, C4) kit consists of one vial each $\mathrm { 5 ~ m L }$ or $2 \mathrm { m L }$ ) of rabbit anti-human C3c and anti-human C4. The antisera contain sodium azide and are supplied as ready-for-use. The assay kit has to be used with the BN™ Systems. N Protein Standard SL (human) and N/T Protein Control SL (L, M and H) (human), N Reaction Buffer and N Diluent are required but not part of the kit.

# L. Test Principle:

The N Antisera to Human Complement Factors (C3c, C4) uses immunonephelometry to measure C3 and C4 in human serum or plasma. C3 or C4 in the sample combines with specific antibodies to C3 or C4 to form immune complexes. These complexes scatter a beam of light passes through the sample. The intensity of the scattered light is proportional to the concentration of the respective analyte in the sample. C3 or C4 concentrations are automatically calculated from multi-point calibration curves.
