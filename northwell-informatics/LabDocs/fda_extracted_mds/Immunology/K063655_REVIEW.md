# L. Test Principle:

Proteins contained in human body fluids form immunochemical reaction with specific antibodies. These complexes scatter a beam o flight passed through the sample. The

intensity of the scattered light is proportional to the concentration of the relevant protein in the sample. The result is evaluated by comparison with a standard of known concentration.
