# H. Intended Use:

1. Intended use(s):

The $\mathfrak { a }$ -Gliatest $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgA is intended for the semi-quantitative determination of IgA antibodies directed against gliadin in human serum. The assay is an aid in the diagnosis of celiac disease and should be used in conjunction with other serological tests and clinical findings.

The $\mathfrak { a }$ -GliatestÒIgG is intended for the semi-quantitative determination of IgG antibodies directed against gliadin in human serum. The assay is an aid in the diagnosis of celiac disease and should be used in conjunction with other serological tests and clinical findings.

The $\mathfrak { a }$ -GliaPep $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgA is intended for the semi-quantitative determination of IgA antibodies directed against deamidated gliadin peptide in human serum. The assay is an aid in the diagnosis of celiac disease and should be used in conjunction with other serological tests and clinical findings.

The $\mathfrak { a }$ -GliaPep $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgG is intended for the semi-quantitative determination of IgG antibodies directed against deamidated gliadin peptide in human serum. The assay is an aid in the diagnosis of celiac disease and should be used in conjunction with other serological tests and clinical findings.

2. Indication(s) for use: Same as Intended Use

3. Special conditions for use statement(s): For prescription use only

# L. Test Principle:

The $\mathfrak { a }$ -Gliatest $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgA and $\mathfrak { a }$ -Gliatest $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgG tests are sandwich type enzyme immunoassays. Purified $\mathfrak { a }$ -gliadin is attached to microwells, and antigen-specific antibodies in the patient serum bind to the antigens. Non-specific antibodies are removed by washing. Horseradish peroxidase labeled goat anti-human IgA or IgG are added and bind to human antibodies in the well. The excess conjugate is washed away; then a chromogenic substrate is added. After an appropriate incubation period, the OD value is measured using an ELISA microtiter plate reader capable of measuring OD at $4 5 0 \mathrm { n m }$ .

The $\mathfrak { a }$ -GliaPep $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgA and $\mathfrak { a }$ -GliaPep $\textsuperscript { \textregistered }$ IgG tests are a sandwich type enzymeimmunoassay. Specific deamidated gliadin peptides are attached to microwells, and antigen-specific antibodies in the patient serum bind to the antigens. Non-specific antibodies are removed by washing. Horseradish peroxidase-labeled goat anti-human IgA is added to bind with human antibodies in the well. The excess conjugate is washed away; then a chromogenic substrate is added. After an appropriate incubation period, the absorbance value is measured by means of an EIA reader $( 4 5 0 \mathrm { n m } )$ .
