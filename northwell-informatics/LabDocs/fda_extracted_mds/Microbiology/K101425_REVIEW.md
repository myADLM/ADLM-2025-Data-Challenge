# H. Intended Use:

1. Intended use:

For use with MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Dried Gram Negative MIC/Combo Panels and Dried Gram Negative Breakpoint Combo Panels. MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ panels are designed for use in determining antimicrobial agent susceptibility and/or identification to the species level of aerobic and facultative anaerobic gram-negative bacilli.

The MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Dried Gram Negative MIC/Combo Panels is used to determine quantitative and/or qualitative antimicrobial agent susceptibility of colonies grown on solid media of rapidly growing aerobic and facultative anaerobic gram negative bacilli.

2. Indication(s) for use:

The MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Dried Gram Negative MIC/Combo Panels is used to determine quantitative and/or qualitative antimicrobial agent susceptibility of colonies grown on solid media of rapidly growing aerobic and facultative anaerobic gram negative bacilli. After incubation, panels are incubated for 16- 20 hours at $3 5 \mathrm { { ^ \circ C } }$ $+ / - 1 ^ { \circ } \mathrm { C }$ in a non- $C 0 _ { 2 }$ incubator, and read either visually or with MicroScan instrumentation, according to the Package insert.

This particular submission is for the addition of Doripenem at concentrations 0.008 to $3 2 \mu \mathrm { g / m L }$ to the test panel.

The gram-negative organisms which may be used for Doripenem susceptibility testing in this panel are:

Acinetobacter baumannii Escherichia coli Klebsiella pneumoniae Proteus mirabilis Pseudomonas aeruginosa

3. Special conditions for use statement(s):

For prescription use only   
The Log and Stationary Inoculum methods should not be used with Doripenem.

4. Special instrument requirements:

MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ WalkAway $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ System and MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ autoSCAN $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ -4 are the alternate read methods for Doripenem

# I. Device Description:

The MicroScan $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ Dried Gram-Negative MIC/Combo Panel contains micro-dilutions of each antimicrobial agent in various concentrations with Mueller Hinton Broth and various nutrients which are dehydrated and dried in panels. Each panel contains two control wells: a no-growth control well (contains water only/no nutrients or broth), and a growth control well (contains test medium without antibiotic). The panel is rehydrated and inoculated at the same time with $0 . 1 \mathrm { m l }$ of suspension prepared by the turbidity method (inoculum prepared in water, then $0 . 1 \mathrm { m l }$ transferred to $2 5 ~ \mathrm { m l }$ of inoculum water containing pluronic-D/F-a wetting solution). The Prompt $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ method of inoculation is also recommended as an alternate means of preparing the inoculum. The panels are incubated at $3 5 \mathrm { { ^ \circ C } }$ in a non- $C 0 _ { 2 }$ for 16- 20 hours and read by visual observation for growth. Panels may also be read automatically with the WalkAway $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ and autoSCAN $^ \mathrm { \textregistered }$ -4 Systems.
