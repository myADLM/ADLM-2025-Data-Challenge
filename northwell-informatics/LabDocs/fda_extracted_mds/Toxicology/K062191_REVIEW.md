Traceability   
The assigned values of the Dimension Vista™ System Drugs of   
Abuse Positive Control and the Dimension Vista™ System Drugs of Abuse Negative Control are traceable to Gas Chromatography / Mass Spectrometry (GC/MS) Reference Testing.

# Value Assignment

Calculated quantities of D-Methamphetamine, Secobarbital, Nordiazepam, Benzoylecgonine, Methadone, Morphine, Phencyclidine, and 11-nor- $. \Delta 9$ -THC9-COOH high purity stock solutions are added to drug free normal human urine to target concentrations for each of the Negative and Positive Controls. The Negative and Positive Bulk Products are tested by GC/MS and any analyte that is out of the acceptable GC/MS range is adjusted and retested. Values are assigned to the controls once the GC/MS results are in the acceptable ranges.
