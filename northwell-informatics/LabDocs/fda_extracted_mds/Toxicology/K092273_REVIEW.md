# Randox Ethanol Assay

The Randox Ethanol Assay is an in vitro diagnostic test for the quantitative analysis of Ethanol in human urine and serum on the Xseries analyzers, which includes the Xdaytona and the Ximola analyzers.

The measurement of ethanol is used for the diagnosis and treatment of alcohol intoxication and poisoning.

This is an in vitro diagnostic device intended for prescription use only.

# Randox Ethanol Calibrator

The Randox Ethanol Calibrator is intended for the calibration of the Randox Ethanol assay, which is used for the quantitative analysis of ethanol in human urine and serum on the Xseries analyzers, which includes the Xdaytona and the Ximola analyzers.

# Randox Ethanol Control

The Randox Ethanol Control Set is intended for the quality control of the Randox Ethanol assay, which is used for the quantitative analysis of ethanol in human urine and serum on the Xseries analyzers, which includes the Xdaytona and the Ximola analyzers.

This is an in vitro diagnostic device intended for prescription use only.

# L. Test Principle:

The RANDOX ethanol assay is a ready-to-use liquid reagent. The assay is based on the oxidation of ethyl alcohol to acetaldehyde by alcohol dehydrogenase (ADH) and NAD to NADH, resulting in an absorbance change measured spectrophotometrically at $3 4 0 \mathrm { n m }$ . The concentration of ethanol alcohol is directly proportional to the ADH activity, measured at $3 4 0 \mathrm { n m }$ wavelength.
